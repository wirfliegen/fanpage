Frequently Asked Questions

What is it about?

This game is Xenoblade 2's prequel which takes place "500 years in the past." There are quotes around that last statement because the game technically takes place 1500+ years in the future if you think about it. The game focuses on Lora and the gang in their battles against Malos, whose goal is to destroy the world.

So this is a prequel to Xenoblade Chronicles 2. Does the order in which you play the games matter?

It doesn't really matter, but some of the events in this game will probably have a greater impact on you if you play Xenoblade 2 first.
